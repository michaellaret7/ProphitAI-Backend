### Description

The edge is liquidity-provision compensation: when uninformed order flow pushes prices away from fundamental anchors, market makers and patient capital earn a premium for taking the other side of temporary mispricing. Unlike raw short-term reversal (which picks up value and size exposures and is highly cost-sensitive), RESIDUAL reversal uses returns already cleansed of market and sector factors, producing a cleaner alpha signal per Da-Liu-Schaumburg 2014 and Blitz Auto-Residual Factor Model 2013.
